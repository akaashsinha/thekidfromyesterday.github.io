Ways to reduce institutional racism

While not a complete list by any means these are many ways to reduce racism

  1. Have tough conversations with yourself and your community on race
    • Having these conversations will not only broaden your perspective but as well as others
    • Condemning racist speech makes it clear to the other person that it is not cool
    • Without this foundation all the other steps are futile
  2. Create an awareness of the issues that the African-American community face
    • Many people are unaware of the struggles face that African Americans in institutional racism
    • When people realize that these problems are not OK, more people will work to fix them
    • Using social media can create an awareness that other generations could have only dreamed of
  3. Converting the awareness into action
    • Once people have become aware of the issues, you have to first organize all of those people before any action is possible
    • Once people are organized they can more effectively put pressure on their elected officials to change their positions on issues that disproportionally hurt African Americans
    • If no one is willing to change their position then the people must take matters into their own hands and run for office themselves
